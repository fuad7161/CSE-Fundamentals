# Relations and Functions

Mathematical descriptions of connections between elements of sets.

- **Relations** — ordered pairs, domain & codomain, inverse relations
- **Properties of Relations** — reflexive, symmetric, antisymmetric, transitive
- **Closures** — reflexive, symmetric, transitive closure (Warshall's algorithm)
- **Equivalence Relations** — equivalence classes, partitions
- **Partial Orders** — posets, Hasse diagrams, total order
- **Functions** — injection (one-to-one), surjection (onto), bijection
- **Composition** — composition of relations and functions, inverse functions
