# Combinatorics and Counting

Techniques for counting arrangements, selections, and probabilities of discrete structures.

- **Counting Principles** — rule of sum, rule of product
- **Permutations** — arrangements without/with repetition, circular permutations
- **Combinations** — selections without/with repetition, binomial coefficients
- **Pigeonhole Principle** — simple and generalized forms
- **Inclusion–Exclusion Principle** — counting unions of overlapping sets
- **Recurrence Relations** — formulation, solving (characteristic equation)
- **Binomial Theorem** — expansion of (x + y)ⁿ, Pascal's triangle
- **Probability Basics** — sample space, events, conditional probability
