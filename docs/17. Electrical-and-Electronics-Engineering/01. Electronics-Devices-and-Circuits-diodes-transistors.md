# Electronics Devices and Circuits — Diodes, Transistors

Fundamental semiconductor devices and their applications in electronic circuits.

- **Semiconductors** — intrinsic vs extrinsic (n-type, p-type), doping
- **Diodes** — p-n junction, forward/reverse bias, VI characteristics
- **Diode Applications** — rectifiers (half-wave, full-wave), clipping, clamping
- **Zener Diode** — breakdown region, voltage regulation
- **Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT)** — NPN/PNP, three regions (cutoff, active, saturation)
- **BJT Configurations** — common-base, common-emitter, common-collector
- **Field-Effect Transistors (FET)** — JFET, MOSFET, enhancement vs depletion mode
- **Amplifiers** — small-signal analysis, biasing, gain
