# Combinational and Sequential Circuits

Two fundamental classes of digital circuits. Covers:

- **Combinational circuits** — output depends only on current inputs (multiplexers, decoders, adders, ALU)
- **Sequential circuits** — output depends on current inputs and past state (flip-flops, registers, counters, memory)
- Clock signal and synchronous vs. asynchronous operation
- Latches (SR, D) vs. flip-flops (edge-triggered)
- Finite State Machines (Mealy vs. Moore)
