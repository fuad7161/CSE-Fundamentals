# Boolean Algebra

Algebraic system for analyzing and simplifying digital logic. Covers:

- Boolean variables and truth values (0, 1)
- Basic operations: AND, OR, NOT, NAND, NOR, XOR, XNOR
- Laws: commutative, associative, distributive, De Morgan's theorems
- Simplification of Boolean expressions
- Duality principle and canonical forms (SOP, POS)
