# Complement System — 1's and 2's Complement

Methods for representing negative numbers in binary. Covers:

- **1's complement** — invert all bits, two representations for zero
- **2's complement** — invert bits and add 1, single representation for zero
- Why 2's complement is preferred (simpler arithmetic, no dual zero)
- Subtraction using complement addition
- Range of representable values in n-bit systems
