# Propositional and Predicate Logic

Formal systems for reasoning about statements and their truth values.

- **Propositional Logic** — atomic & compound propositions, truth tables
- **Logical Connectives** — negation, conjunction, disjunction, implication, biconditional
- **Equivalences** — tautology, contradiction, logical equivalence, laws (distributive, De Morgan's)
- **Normal Forms** — DNF, CNF
- **Predicate Logic** — quantifiers (universal ∀, existential ∃), predicates
- **Inference Rules** — modus ponens, modus tollens, resolution
- **Proof Techniques** — direct proof, proof by contradiction, proof by induction
