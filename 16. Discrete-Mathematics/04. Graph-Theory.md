# Graph Theory

Study of graphs — structures of vertices connected by edges — and their properties.

- **Basic Definitions** — vertices, edges, directed vs undirected graphs, weighted graphs
- **Graph Representations** — adjacency matrix, adjacency list
- **Types of Graphs** — complete, bipartite, planar, tree, multigraph
- **Paths & Cycles** — Eulerian paths/circuits, Hamiltonian paths/cycles
- **Graph Connectivity** — strongly connected, weakly connected, cut vertices/bridges
- **Spanning Trees** — minimum spanning tree (Kruskal's, Prim's algorithms)
- **Coloring** — vertex coloring, chromatic number, four-color theorem
- **Shortest Path** — Dijkstra's, Bellman-Ford algorithms
