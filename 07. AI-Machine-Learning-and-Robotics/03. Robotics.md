# Robotics

Introduction to the design, construction, operation, and application of robots.

- **Definition** — interdisciplinary field combining mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science
- **Robot components**: sensors, actuators, controllers, power supply
- **Types of robots**: industrial, service, mobile, autonomous, humanoid
- **Sensors** — cameras, LIDAR, ultrasonic, encoders, IMUs
- **Actuators** — motors, servos, hydraulic/pneumatic systems
- **Control architectures** — reactive, deliberative, hybrid (e.g., ROS)
- **Applications** — manufacturing, healthcare, exploration, defense, household
