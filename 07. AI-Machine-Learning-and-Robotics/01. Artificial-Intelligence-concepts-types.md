# Artificial Intelligence — Concepts & Types

Brief description of the foundational ideas behind AI, its evolution, and its major classifications.

- **Definition of AI** — simulating human intelligence in machines
- **Narrow AI (Weak AI)** — specialized for one task (e.g., chess engines, voice assistants)
- **General AI (Strong AI)** — hypothetical machine with human-like cognitive abilities
- **Superintelligence** — AI surpassing human intelligence across all domains
- **Key subfields**: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Robotics
