# E-Mail — SMTP, POP, IMAP

Protocols that enable electronic mail delivery and retrieval. Covers:

- **SMTP** — sending outgoing mail (push protocol)
- **POP3** — retrieving and deleting mail from server (offline access)
- **IMAP** — managing mail on the server (online/offline sync, folders)
- MIME attachments and encoding
- Comparison of POP3 vs. IMAP use cases
