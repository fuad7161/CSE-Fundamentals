# Computer Maintenance

Best practices for keeping computer hardware and software in good working order.

- **Hardware maintenance**: cleaning components, thermal paste, cable management
- **Software maintenance**: OS updates, driver updates, patches
- **Disk maintenance**: defragmentation (HDD), TRIM (SSD), disk cleanup
- **Security maintenance**: antivirus scans, firewall configuration, backup routines
- **Preventive maintenance**: surge protection, proper ventilation, regular inspections
- Troubleshooting common issues: slow performance, overheating, boot failures
