## Suggested Tagging Convention
`[ChapterCode]-[SubtopicCode]-[ContentType]`
Example: `10.2-MCQ-01`, `8.4-THEORY`, `1.3-WRITTEN-Q`

## Suggested Content Types per Topic
- **THEORY** — 150–300 word concise explanation
- **WRITTEN** — 1–2 short-answer or descriptive questions (bank exam style, 3–5 marks)
- **MCQ** — 5–10 MCQs per subtopic, with one being a "trap" option (common wrong answer bank exams use)

## Note on BB/BCS Exam Pattern
Bangladesh government exams (BB AD, BCS, Combined Bank) tend to over-index on:
- Number system conversions
- Storage unit conversions (KB→MB→GB→TB, powers of 2)
- Full forms/abbreviations (RAM, ROM, BIOS, USB, HTTP, etc.)
- "Who invented/first used X" trivia
- OS & software classification (System vs Application)
- Virus/malware terminology distinctions

Consider tagging these "high-yield" subtopics separately so your generation system prioritizes MCQ volume there over pure theory depth.
