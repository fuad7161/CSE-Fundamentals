# History of Computer — Beginning of IT

The evolution of computing from ancient calculating tools to modern digital systems.

### 1.2 — Evolution of Computers (Generations)
- 1st Gen (1940–1956): Vacuum tubes, ENIAC, UNIVAC
- 2nd Gen (1956–1963): Transistors, magnetic core memory
- 3rd Gen (1964–1971): ICs, IBM System/360
- 4th Gen (1971–present): Microprocessors, VLSI, personal computers
- 5th Gen (present & beyond): AI, quantum computing, ULSI

### 11.4 — Notable Computer Pioneers & Their Contributions
- Charles Babbage — Father of the Computer (Analytical Engine)
- Ada Lovelace — First programmer
- Alan Turing — Turing Machine, AI concept
- John von Neumann — Von Neumann architecture
- John Atanasoff, Howard Aiken, Grace Hopper, Steve Jobs, Bill Gates
