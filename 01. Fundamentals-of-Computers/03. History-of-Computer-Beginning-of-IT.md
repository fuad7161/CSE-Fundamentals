# History of Computer — Beginning of IT

A chronological overview of the evolution of computing devices from early mechanical calculators to modern digital computers and the dawn of information technology.

- Early computing devices (abacus, Pascal's calculator, Babbage's Analytical Engine)
- Generations of computers (vacuum tubes to AI)
- Key figures (Ada Lovelace, Alan Turing, John von Neumann)
- Milestones in the history of IT
