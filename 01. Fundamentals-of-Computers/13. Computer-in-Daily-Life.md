# Computer in Daily Life

How computers and computing technology impact everyday life across industries and personal activities.

### 1.7 — Computer in Daily Life
- Banking: online banking, ATMs, NEFT/RTGS, mobile banking
- Education: e-learning, LMS (Moodle), digital classrooms, research
- Healthcare: electronic health records, diagnostic systems, telemedicine
- Government services: e-Governance, digital Bangladesh initiative
- e-Commerce: online shopping (Daraz, Amazon), payment gateways
- Communication: email, instant messaging, VoIP (Zoom, Skype)
- Entertainment: streaming (YouTube, Netflix), gaming, music
- Transportation: GPS, ride-sharing (Uber/Pathao), airline reservation
- Smart homes: IoT, automation, smart appliances
