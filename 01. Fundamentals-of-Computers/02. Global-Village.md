## Supplementary Notes from Master Study Guide

### Internet Services
- **Email:** Electronic mail — global messaging
- **Online Chat:** Real-time text communication
- **Online Shopping:** E-commerce and digital transactions
- **Usenet:** Distributed newsgroups and discussion forums
- **WWW (World Wide Web):** Hyperlinked information system

### E-Business Categories (Global Commerce)

| Category | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| B2B | Business to Business | Supplier to manufacturer |
| B2C | Business to Consumer | Online retail (Amazon) |
| C2C | Consumer to Consumer | eBay, OLX |
| C2B | Consumer to Business | Google Adsense, freelance platforms |

### Internet Timeline (Key Milestones)
- **1969:** ARPAnet established (first packet-switching network)
- **1974:** TCP/IP developed (foundation of internet)
- **1984:** DNS introduced (domain name system)
- **1992:** World Wide Web made public
