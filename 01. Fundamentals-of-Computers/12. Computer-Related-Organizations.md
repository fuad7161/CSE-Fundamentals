# Computer-Related Organizations

Major international and national organizations that set standards, regulate protocols, and advance the computing industry.

### 11.1 — International Bodies
- IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) — standards (Wi-Fi 802.11)
- ACM (Association for Computing Machinery) — computing research
- ISO (International Organization for Standardization) — OSI model, quality standards
- W3C (World Wide Web Consortium) — web standards (HTML, CSS)
- ICANN (Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers) — domain names, IP allocation

### 11.2 — Bangladesh-specific Bodies
- BASIS (Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services) — IT industry body
- BCC (Bangladesh Computer Council) — government IT policy and training
- ICT Division — government ministry overseeing ICT development
- BCS (Bangladesh Computer Society) — professional membership organization

### 11.3 — Standards & Protocols Bodies
- IETF (Internet Engineering Task Force) — internet protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP)
- ITU (International Telecommunication Union) — telecom standards, 4G/5G
- ANSI (American National Standards Institute) — US standards coordination
- NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) — cybersecurity framework
