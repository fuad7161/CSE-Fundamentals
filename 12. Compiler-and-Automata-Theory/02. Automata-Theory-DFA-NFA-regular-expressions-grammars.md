# Automata Theory: DFA, NFA, Regular Expressions, and Grammars

Covers the theoretical foundations of computation — finite automata, formal languages, and grammars.

- **DFA (Deterministic Finite Automaton):** definition, transition diagrams, acceptance of strings
- **NFA (Nondeterministic Finite Automaton):** epsilon transitions, NFA to DFA conversion
- **Regular expressions:** operators, equivalence to finite automata
- **Regular languages:** closure properties and pumping lemma
- **Context-free grammars (CFG):** productions, derivations, parse trees
- **Pushdown automata (PDA):** relationship with context-free languages
- Chomsky hierarchy: Type-0 through Type-3 grammars
