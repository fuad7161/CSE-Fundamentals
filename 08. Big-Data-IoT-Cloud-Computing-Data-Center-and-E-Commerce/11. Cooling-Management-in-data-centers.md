# Cooling Management in Data Centers

Techniques and strategies for thermal management to prevent equipment overheating.

- **Importance** — servers generate significant heat; excessive heat reduces reliability and lifespan
- **Cooling methods**:
  - **Air cooling** — CRAC/CRAH units, hot-aisle/cold-aisle containment, raised floor
  - **Liquid cooling** — direct-to-chip, immersion cooling, rear-door heat exchangers
  - **Free cooling** — using outside air when ambient temperature is low
- **Key metrics**: return temperature, supply temperature, PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness)
- **Best practices**: blanking panels, cable management, humidity control, airflow optimization
- **Emerging trends**: AI-driven cooling, liquid cooling for high-density GPU clusters
