# E-Commerce

Overview of electronic commerce, its business models, and underlying technologies.

- **Definition** — buying and selling goods/services over the internet
- **Business models**:
  - B2C (Business-to-Consumer) — e.g., Amazon, Walmart
  - B2B (Business-to-Business) — e.g., Alibaba, SAP Ariba
  - C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer) — e.g., eBay, Etsy
  - C2B (Consumer-to-Business) — e.g., freelance platforms
- **Key components**: online storefront, payment gateway, shopping cart, inventory management
- **Payment systems**: credit cards, digital wallets (PayPal, Stripe), cryptocurrencies
- **Security**: SSL/TLS, PCI-DSS compliance, fraud detection
- **Trends**: mobile commerce, social commerce, omnichannel retail, AI-powered recommendations
