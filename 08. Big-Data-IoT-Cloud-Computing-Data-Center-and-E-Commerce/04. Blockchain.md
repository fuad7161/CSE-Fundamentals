# Blockchain

Introduction to distributed ledger technology and its core principles.

- **Definition** — decentralized, immutable ledger that records transactions across a peer-to-peer network
- **Key concepts**: blocks, chain, hashing, consensus mechanisms
- **Consensus algorithms**: Proof of Work (PoW), Proof of Stake (PoS), Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance (PBFT)
- **Smart contracts** — self-executing code deployed on the blockchain
- **Types**: public (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum), private, consortium
- **Applications**: cryptocurrencies, supply chain, voting, identity management, NFTs
- **Challenges**: scalability, energy consumption, regulatory uncertainty
